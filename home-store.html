Frequently Asked Questions
Discover Valuable Insights in Our FAQs - Your Common Questions, Expertly Answered.
How does HomeLink work?

Visitors scan the HomeLink QR code placed at your doorstep. This action instantly notifies you and initiates a video or audio call, connecting you with the visitor.

Do I need to install any software to use HomeLink?

Homeowners need to download the Codebell app from PlayStore or AppStore to receive calls, manage settings, and use additional features.

Do visitors need to download any software/App to use HomeLink QR at my doorstep ?

No, visitors do not need to download or install any app to use HomeLink QR. They can simply use smartphone camera or any QR scanner to scan HomeLink QR and initiate a call.

Can HomeLink work without internet?

Internet connection is required on both the visitor’s and the homeowner’s mobile devices to conduct video/audio calls. It can be either mobile data or wifi.

Is HomeLink secure?

Yes, HomeLink is designed with privacy and security in mind. Calls are encrypted, and no personal phone numbers are shared between the homeowner and the visitor.

What happens if I miss a call from a visitor?

If you miss a call, HomeLink allows you to view call logs and even call back missed visitors, ensuring you never miss an important visit.

Can HomeLink replace my traditional doorbell?

HomeLink is intended to complement, not replace, your traditional doorbell. It provides additional, smart capabilities like video/audio communication and call logs, all this while being away from home. As such use doorbell while at home and Codebell while away from Home.

How is HomeLink powered?

HomeLink does not require any external power source or batteries, as it primarily uses QR code technology and mobile data for initiating calls.

Are there any recurring subscriptions or payments ?

There are no recurring charges to be paid . Non-business Customers need to pay only one time cost for HomeLinkQR .

Is HomeLink a CCTV Camera ?

No , its not a CCTV camera. Neither does it serve as an alternative for CCTV camera.

I already have a CCTV camera and I can find out who visited my premises. Why would I need HomeLink ?

CCTV doesn’t send you an alert and there is no way to find out when a visitor arrives. You will need to check the entire day footage to find out who all visited. This is neither practical not recommended.

Can I switch off geo-fencing ?

Yes, you may chose to switch off geo-fencing in case GPS location is not accurate in your area.
