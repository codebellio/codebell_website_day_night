Frequently Asked Questions
Discover Valuable Insights in Our FAQs - Your Common Questions, Expertly Answered.
Is an app required by passerby, traffic police to use CarConnect?

No, an app is not required. CarConnect is designed to be user-friendly and accessible to everyone. You can use any QR scanner or camera-equipped device to interact with CarConnect.

Will I get notifications automatically if I have parked the vehicle in no parking or wrong parking?

You will not be automatically notified of wrong parking. It is only when someone scans the CarConnect on your vehicle and calls you, you will be informed of the wrong parking.

Can I track the current location of my vehicle ?

No, you cannot track current location of your vehicle using CarConnect. Since it does not include a GPS tracker device, it cannot provide real time location updates.

What happens if I receive a call while away from my phone?

With CarConnect's call forwarding feature, calls are automatically redirected to pre-set devices, ensuring you never miss an important call. Share your CarConnect QR with someone who can handle the issue in your absence.

How does the Last Scan Location feature work?

The Last Scan Location feature helps you locate your parked car easily. Simply scan the Codebell QR when you park your car, and CarConnect will guide you back using live GPS location tracking.

I don’t drive my Car, I have a driver. Can I assign CarConnect to my driver?

Yes, absolutely! If you have a driver who handles your vehicle, you can easily assign the CarConnect QR to your driver’s mobile device. All calls related to your car will be directly received by the assigned driver.

Is CarConnect compatible with all types of vehicles?

Yes, CarConnect is compatible with all types of vehicles, regardless of make or model. Whether you drive a car, SUV, truck, or motorcycle, CarConnect offers seamless assistance and communication features.

Is CarConnect secure?

Yes, CarConnect prioritizes user security and privacy. Calls are encrypted, and no personal phone numbers are shared between the carowner and the caller.

I own multiple cars. Can I connect my phone to all my cars ?

Yes, you can connect as many CarConenct QRs to one single device. You will receive calls from different cars on your phone with distinct identifier for each call.

Is Geo-fencing applicable on CarConnect ?

No, geo-fencing is not applicable for CarConnect since car is a moving asset and keep changing location.
